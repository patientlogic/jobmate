# JobMate - Job Search Assistant

## <a href="https://demo.JobMate.ca">Live Demo</a>

JobMate is a web app companion for managing your job search journey. This free and open-source project is designed to help job seekers efficiently track and organize their job applications. Say goodbye to the chaos of scattered information and hello to a streamlined, intuitive, and powerful job search experience running locally on your system.

Job searching can be overwhelming, with numerous applications to track and deadlines to meet. JobSeeker Assistant is here to simplify this process, allowing you to focus on big picture and keep track of your job search related activities. JobMate app platform empowers you with the tools you need to stay organized, informed, and proactive throughout your job search.
